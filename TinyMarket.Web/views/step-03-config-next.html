You can specify which step will be navigated when user clicks "Next" button in your step's controller.

Check this box on and press "Next" you will skip the next step.

Note the the "Previous" button is smart enough to back to the previous step you visited.

You can specify whether the "Finish" button should be shown.

Check this box on you will see "Finish" button.

Wizard will show "Finish" button on its last step even though you spcified not to show.
